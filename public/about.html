I wanted become software engineer Because, A software engineer is a professional who applies engineering principles to the design, development, testing, and maintenance of software. They work to create software that is reliable, efficient, and meets the needs of users and clients. Software engineers may be involved in software creation for various applications, from operating systems and network control systems to business applications and video games. Here are some key aspects of software engineering: Modularity: Breaking down a large software project into smaller, manageable pieces.

Abstraction: Focusing on the essential features of a component while ignoring its complexities.

Encapsulation: Protecting the internal state of an object from outside interference.

Maintenance: Regularly updating software to improve performance, correct errors, and adapt to new requirements.

Testing: Ensuring the software functions correctly and meets its specifications

I immersed myself in the wonders of Dubai, a city that dances between the future and tradition. My journey began at the Burj Khalifa, the towering sentinel that watches over the city.
